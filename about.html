    Our History, Mission & Spiritual Foundation

    Our Story

    St. Francis of Assisi Anglican Church has been a beacon of faith and community in Mamelodi West for decades. Named after the beloved saint who championed simplicity, poverty, and devotion to God's creation, our church embodies these timeless values in service to our congregation and community.

    Our building stands as a testament to the faith of countless worshippers who have walked through its doors seeking solace, strength, and spiritual connection. From humble beginnings, we have grown into a vibrant, inclusive community that welcomes all who seek to grow in their faith and serve others.

    We are proud to be part of the global Anglican Communion, preserving the rich traditions of worship while embracing contemporary approaches to ministry and community engagement.

    Our Core Values

    Compassion

    We serve with love and empathy, caring for all God's children with dignity and respect.

    Devotion

    Our faith is the foundation of everything we do, guiding us in worship and service.

    Community

    We believe in the strength of united faith and collective action for the common good.

    Stewardship

    Following St. Francis's example, we cherish and protect God's creation.

    Knowledge

    We promote spiritual education and understanding of Scripture and Anglican tradition.

    Faith

    Our unwavering belief in Jesus Christ and the Gospel shapes all that we do.

    Our Vision for the Future

    We envision St. Francis Church as a thriving sanctuary where people of all backgrounds encounter God's grace, grow in their faith, and are empowered to serve their communities with joy, compassion, and purpose.
