    12 Ways to Serve, Grow & Connect

    We offer diverse programs for all ages and backgrounds. Whether you seek spiritual growth, community service, fellowship, or a deeper connection to God, there's a place for you at St. Francis.

    Choir Ministry

    Leads worship with music, blending tradition and contemporary Anglican hymns.

    Youth Ministry

    Fellowship, service, and spiritual growth for ages 13–25.

    Community Outreach

    Serving neighbors through food, clothing, and support programs.

    Mother's Union

    Supporting families and Christian values through prayer and outreach.

    Women's Fellowship

    Spiritual growth, fellowship, and service for women of all ages.

    Bernard Mizeki Men's Guild

    Fellowship and service for men in the parish, inspired by Saint Bernard Mizeki.

    Anglican Men's Guild

    Encouraging men to grow in faith and serve the church with strength and purpose.

    Lay Ministers

    Assisting worship, pastoral care, and supporting clergy in all church ministries.

    St. Agnes

    Nurturing young girls in faith, leadership, and service to their communities.

    St. Mary Magdalene

    Spiritual growth and fellowship for women, inspired by St. Mary Magdalene's devotion.

    Women of Charity

    Acts of kindness and service for those in need within and beyond our parish.

    Sunday School

    Fun, nurturing environment for children to learn about God and grow in faith.

    Join a Ministry Today

    Everyone has gifts and talents to share. Whether you're new to the church or a longtime member, we invite you to discover how you can use your talents to serve God and strengthen our community.
