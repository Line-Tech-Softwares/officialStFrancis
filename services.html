    Sacraments & Pastoral Services

    Baptism

    The sacrament of initiation into the Christian faith. Baptism welcomes individuals into God's family and the church community. Contact us to arrange a baptism service.

    Holy Matrimony

    We celebrate the sacred bond of marriage within the context of Christian faith and community. Our clergy are available for marriage counseling and ceremony planning.

    Confirmation

    A mature affirmation of baptismal vows. Confirmation classes prepare candidates to publicly claim their Christian faith. Classes held throughout the year.

    Holy Communion

    The Eucharist lies at the heart of Anglican worship. We celebrate this sacrament at every service, welcoming all baptized Christians to receive.

    Reconciliation

    Private confession is available by appointment with our clergy. This ancient practice offers healing and spiritual direction.

    Last Rites

    Pastoral care and sacraments offered to the dying and their families. We provide spiritual comfort during life's final moments.

    Special Services & Events

    Throughout the liturgical year, we celebrate significant holy days and seasons with special services and events.

    • Easter Vigil & Sunrise Service - Celebrate the Resurrection
    • Christmas Eve Candlelit Service - Experience the wonder of Advent
    • Good Friday Stations of the Cross - Meditative journey through Passion
    • Ash Wednesday Service - Begin the Lenten season with prayer
    • All Saints' Day Remembrance - Honor those who have gone before us
    • St. Francis Feast Day (October 4th) - Celebration of our patron saint
