    Leadership, Organization & Governance

    Church Leadership & Organization

    St. Francis Church is led by a dedicated team of clergy and lay leaders committed to serving our congregation and fulfilling our mission with integrity, transparency, and Christ-centered values.

    Clergy

    Revd Canon Benjamin Moleko

    Rector

    Spiritual leader of the parish with responsibility for worship, pastoral care, and overall ministry. Provides guidance and sacramental leadership to our community.

    Deacon Charles Moeng

    Associate Priest

    Assists with worship services, pastoral visits, and ministry coordination. Focuses on youth engagement and community outreach initiatives.

    Wardens & Vestry

    Mr. Motlatsi Mabena

    Senior Warden

    Oversees church property and finances in partnership with the Rector. Manages maintenance projects and long-term facility planning.

    Mrs. Sophie Mampuru

    Junior Warden

    Assists with property matters and coordinates lay ministry initiatives. Oversees volunteer scheduling and event coordination.

    Mr. David Phakamani

    Treasurer

    Manages finances, budgets, and financial accountability. Ensures transparent financial reporting and responsible stewardship of resources.

    Mr. Michael Modise

    Alternate Warden

    Supports both Senior and Junior Wardens in their duties. Acts as a liaison between the vestry and various ministry committees.

    Ministry Leaders

    Mrs. Peggy Kambule

    Stewardship Coordinator

    Leads the church's stewardship program, encouraging generous giving of time, talent, and treasure. Organizes annual pledge campaigns and educates members on biblical giving principles.

    Ms. Ruth Masbaba

    Social Development Coordinator

    Oversees programs addressing community needs such as food security, counseling services, and skills development workshops for vulnerable members of our community.

    Mr. William Makgatho

    Caretaker

    Ensures our church facilities are clean, safe, and well-maintained. Coordinates repairs, manages supplies, and prepares spaces for worship services and events.

    Mrs. Martha Mokoena

    Community Outreach Coordinator

    Coordinates charitable programs and community service initiatives, building bridges between the church and the wider Mamelodi West community.

    Our Governance Principles

    Transparency

    We operate with openness and accountability in all financial and administrative matters.

    Inclusivity

    All voices are valued; we make decisions together as a faith community.

    Justice

    We uphold fairness, equity, and Christian principles in all governance decisions.

    Tradition

    We honor Anglican heritage while remaining responsive to contemporary needs.

    Vision

    We plan strategically to fulfill our mission and serve future generations.

    Service

    Everything we do is guided by Christ's call to love and serve one another.
