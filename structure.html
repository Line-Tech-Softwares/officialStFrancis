    Leadership, Organization & Governance

    Church Leadership & Organization

    St. Francis Church is led by a dedicated team of clergy and lay leaders committed to serving our congregation and fulfilling our mission with integrity, transparency, and Christ-centered values.

    Clergy

    Reverend Dr. Samuel Njoko

    Rector

    Spiritual leader of the parish with responsibility for worship, pastoral care, and overall ministry.

    Reverend Thabo Mthembu

    Associate Priest

    Assists with worship services, pastoral visits, and ministry coordination.

    Wardens & Vestry

    Mr. Jabulani Khumalo

    Senior Warden

    Oversees church property and finances in partnership with the Rector.

    Mrs. Nomsa Dlamini

    Junior Warden

    Assists with property matters and coordinates lay ministry initiatives.

    Mr. David Phakamani

    Treasurer

    Manages finances, budgets, and financial accountability.

    Ministry Leaders

    Mrs. Grace Okonkwo

    Director of Music & Choir

    Leads the choir ministry and oversees all music programs.

    Mr. Sipho Nkosi

    Youth Ministry Coordinator

    Develops and leads programs for young people ages 13-25.

    Mrs. Martha Mokoena

    Community Outreach Director

    Coordinates charitable programs and community service.

    Our Governance Principles

    Transparency

    We operate with openness and accountability in all financial and administrative matters.

    Inclusivity

    All voices are valued; we make decisions together as a faith community.

    Justice

    We uphold fairness, equity, and Christian principles in all governance decisions.

    Tradition

    We honor Anglican heritage while remaining responsive to contemporary needs.

    Vision

    We plan strategically to fulfill our mission and serve future generations.

    Service

    Everything we do is guided by Christ's call to love and serve one another.
